About ITCA

Our Heritage The Project Build Trust has been building educational buildings in KwaZulu-Natal for almost 40 years and has built over 5300 classrooms to date. It has its roots in the Urban Foundationâ€™s classroom building division, founded in 1977. When the trustees of the foundation believed it had served its purpose in 1994, the Natal School Project was formed as a separate trust, which became the Project Build Trust. There is hardly a community in KwaZulu-Natal that has not been touched by our work, which also includes the building of community centres and other humanitarian buildings.
